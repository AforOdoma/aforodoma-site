Key Performance Indicators (KPI) Dashboards

A beautiful feature about having KPI Dashboards is the power it provides organizations to customize their unique KPI Goals at a much granular level. To do this, Click on ‘Set KPI Goals’. On the 'Set KPI Goals' tab we have a couple of services customized with sliders. So I can conveniently move the sliders to customize KPI goals and meet my unique targets. Default percentage of S3 standard is less than or equal to 65%. Meaning that the goal is to have 65% or less of my S3 buckets spend be on S3 standard buckets. So, if I want to have 55% or less of my S3 buckets spend be on S3 standard buckets, I can simply return to the 'Set KPI Goals' tab and move the slider accordingly. .
